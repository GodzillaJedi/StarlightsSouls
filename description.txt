The addon for Fargos's Souls. The main goal of the mod is to balance many global mods with Fargos and among themselves.


Source code: https://github.com/Shtuks/StarlightsSouls

Discord Server: https://discord.gg/xB9s8SyQJr

Official Wiki: https://terrariamods.wiki.gg/wiki/Community_Souls_Expansion


All things mod doing for now:

- Souls for Clicker and Beekeeper Class

- Force for Calamity Addons

- Overloaded bosses summons and energizers for SoA, Calamity, Thorium

- Merge kinetic(SoA) thrower(Thorium) and rogue(Calamity) in one class

- Reworked boots and shields progression

- Adds SoA and Thorium items fot 1st tier souls recepies and effects

- Redemption, Thorium, SoA caught NPCs

- Thorium/SoA/Polarities/Spooky enchantments, forces, souls

Full list of supported mods: Calamity, Shadows of Abaddon, Thorium, Redemption, Spooky, Polarities, Bombus Apis, Clicker Class
Coming soon compatibility: Homeward Journey, Spirit, Consolaria
