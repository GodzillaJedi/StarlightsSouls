The addon for Fargos's souls mod.

Source code: https://github.com/Shtuks/StarlightsSouls

Discord Server: https://discord.gg/xB9s8SyQJr

The content:

- Old Calamity DLC balance (switchable)

- Calamity swarms, caught NPCs, singularities

- Thorium/Shadows of Abaddon enchantments, forces, souls, swarms

- Redemption/Calamity enchantments, forces, souls (Work in progress)

- Post-Mutant Extra content (Echdeath, Shtuxibus, Duke Fishron EX)
